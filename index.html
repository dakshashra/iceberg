OUR VISION
"There are more things in heaven and earth, Horatio, than are dreamt of in your philosophy."
-Shakespeare, Hamlet.
At a time when bi-multilingual education continues to build momentum as a field of research, practice, and advocacy across the globe, there is a need for a concerted strategic vision and approach for prompting the field's systematic internationalization.

As scholars committed to the advancement of bi-multilingual education within and beyond our respective national contexts (e.g., Brasil, España, the United States of America), we are extremely excited and honored to welcome you to ICBERG, the International and Comparative Bi-Multilingual Education Research Group.

We envision ICBERG as a unifying international movement and community of mindful, open-minded, engaged, and collaborative scholar-practitioners dedicated to forging a new transnational path for bilingual education research, practice, professional development, and advocacy.

From a variety of conceptual, theoretical, and research traditions and world-contexts―and through international and comparative bilingual education research, professional development and advocacy―ICBERG aspires to serve as a steadfast driving force for fostering partnerships, building and nurturing collaborations, and generating globally minded innovations for making a greater impact within, across, and beyond national borders.

We warmly welcome you to this exciting adventure aimed at building a world-community and broadening epistemic horizons in bi-multilingual education research, scholarship, practice, and advocacy!

Cristian, Antonieta, and Marisa
OUR PRINCIPLED APPROACH TO ENGAGEMENT

Currently, ICBERG comprises an interdisciplinary Board of Distinguished Advisors and a team of expert international Scholar-Members. Through creating conditions for engendering a “beloved community” (Josiah Joyce & Martin Luther King) of mindful collaborators, ICBERG is poised to fostering a joyful and ethical network of engaged scholar-practitioners through authentic, purposeful, meaningful, transparent, and productive partnerships and collaborations.
